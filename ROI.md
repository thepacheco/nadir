# Nadir — Return on Investment (ROI)

Nadir transforms siloed, stagnant company data into a live, kinetic operational graph. By visualizing data over your physical footprint and automating the escalation of bottlenecks, Nadir delivers immediate, measurable Return on Investment across three core pillars.

## 1. Automated Predictive Bidding & Cost Mapping
* **The Problem:** Companies manually estimate production costs for large bids (e.g., airline catering, manufacturing contracts) using outdated Excel sheets, leading to margin erosion.
* **The Nadir Solution:** Nadir ingests your historical cycle data and maps the micro-costs of every process node (e.g., slicing an apple, packaging, labor time).
* **The ROI:** **Increased Profit Margins.** By knowing the exact cost of production down to the second and the cent, your company can bid aggressively and accurately, winning more contracts without sacrificing margins.

## 2. Frictionless Escalation & Uptime
* **The Problem:** When an actuator fails or a crew is short, the problem sits in an inbox or a Slack channel for hours before reaching the right decision-maker.
* **The Nadir Solution:** The unified Org Tree and Kanban Ticketing system instantly routes anomalies to the correct manager. If a manager is on PTO, Nadir automatically routes the ticket up the chain of command.
* **The ROI:** **Massive Reduction in Downtime.** Faster resolution times mean less time machines are offline and less time staff are sitting idle. A 10% reduction in downtime on a manufacturing floor pays for the Nadir platform in a matter of days.

## 3. Data Consolidation & Executive Visibility
* **The Problem:** The C-Suite spends millions on disparate BI tools, ERPs, and compliance monitors, requiring teams of analysts to build weekly reports.
* **The Nadir Solution:** Nadir acts as the "operating system" for your business, sitting on top of your existing databases (SAP, EBS, SQL). It provides a live, 2D blueprint of your facilities overlaid with real-time operational alerts and a unified macro-dashboard.
* **The ROI:** **Elimination of Software Bloat.** Consolidate your BI, mapping, and ticketing systems into one pane of glass, dramatically reducing software licensing costs and freeing up analyst hours.
